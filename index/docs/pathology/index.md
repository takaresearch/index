# PATHOLOGY

本セクションでは、足部・足関節の主要な病態について、解剖学的部位別（後足部、中足部、前足部)に整理しています。各病態は頻度順に配列されており、非手術的治療と手術的治療の両方について記載しています。

## Overview

This section provides comprehensive information on common foot and ankle pathologies, organized by anatomical region:

- **Hindfoot**: Conditions affecting the ankle and heel
- **Midfoot**: Conditions affecting the arch and midfoot joints  
- **Forefoot**: Conditions affecting the toes and metatarsals

For each pathology, both non-surgical and surgical management approaches are covered.

## Organization

Each pathology is organized by frequency within its anatomical region and includes:

- Clinical presentation and diagnosis
- Non-surgical treatment options
- Surgical indications and techniques
- Evidence-based management strategies

## Clinical Application

This resource is designed for:

- Quick reference during clinical encounters
- Treatment planning and decision-making
- Patient education and counseling
- Preparing for case presentations
