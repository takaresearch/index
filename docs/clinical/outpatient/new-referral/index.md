# New Referral

First-time consultations for patients referred to the foot and ankle service. The approach differs based on whether surgical intervention is being considered.

## A. Diagnostic Testing + Non-Surgical Treatment

For patients requiring:
- Investigation to confirm diagnosis
- Conservative management trial
- Baseline assessment before deciding treatment escalation

**Key Goals**:
- Establish working diagnosis
- Order appropriate investigations
- Initiate conservative treatment
- Plan review timeline with clear decision points

---

## B. Surgical Treatment

For patients where:
- Diagnosis is established
- Conservative treatment has failed or is inappropriate
- Surgical intervention is being offered

**Key Goals**:
- Confirm surgical indication
- Discuss risks, benefits, and recovery
- Obtain informed consent
- Plan perioperative pathway
