# OUT PATIENT

This section provides structured templates for outpatient consultations, organized by patient type and clinical scenario. Each scenario includes guidance for patient communication, documentation, and presentation.

## Structure

### 1. New Referral
First-time consultations for new patients referred to the foot and ankle service.

- **A. Diagnostic Testing + Non-Surgical Treatment**: Patients requiring investigation with planned conservative management
- **B. Surgical Treatment**: Patients being offered operative intervention

### 2. Follow-up
Return visits for established patients.

- **A. Test Results + Non-Surgical Treatment**: Reviewing investigations and initiating/continuing conservative care
- **B. Post-Op Acute (Complication Monitoring)**: Early postoperative review (0-6 weeks) focusing on wound healing and complication surveillance
- **C. Post-Op Subacute (Functional Recovery)**: Later postoperative review (6 weeks+) focusing on rehabilitation and functional outcomes

## Format

Each clinical scenario is presented in three formats:

1. **Patient Communication**: Conversational phrases and explanation templates for direct patient interaction
2. **Documentation**: Structured templates for medical records and referral letters
3. **Short Presentation**: Concise format for presenting to colleagues or at handover

## Components

Each format follows the same clinical structure:

- **a. History Taking**: Key questions and information gathering
- **b. Examination**: Physical findings and assessment
- **c. Impression**: Diagnostic synthesis and clinical reasoning
- **d. Recommendation**: Treatment plan and follow-up strategy
