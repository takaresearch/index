### Initial Greeting & Verification

#### Self-Introduction
- **"Hello, nice to meet you. I am Dr. Taka, the fellow working with Dr. Glazebrook."**
  - Used when meeting a patient for the first time

#### Patient Information Verification
- **"Could you please confirm your full name and date of birth for me? Thank you."**
  - Standard safety check before any clinical encounter

#### Chief Complaint
- **"What brings you into the clinic today?"**
  - Open-ended question to understand the main reason for visit

#### Past Medical History
- **"Do you have any past medical history, such as diabetes?"**
  - Screening for relevant medical conditions that may affect treatment

#### Current Medications
- **"Are you currently taking any medications?"**
  - Important for drug interactions and understanding current treatment
